Discover the hidden rhythms of your body and mind with the "Biorhythms" app. Our advanced tool helps you decode your internal biological clock, predict energy peaks and drops, and align your daily activities with your natural flows.

Unlike outdated trackers, we monitor 4 key life cycles based on precise mathematical models:
• Physical (23 days) — stamina, energy, strength, and coordination.
• Emotional (28 days) — mood, sensitivity, creativity, and empathy.
• Intellectual (33 days) — logic, concentration, memory, and learning capacity.
• Intuitive (38 days) — sixth sense, unconscious insights, and split-second decisions.
